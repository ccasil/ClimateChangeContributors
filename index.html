Climate Change Contributors



Story line: We aim to display the contributing factors of Climate Change. The X axis displays the GDP, while the Y Axis will change depending on the input selected. This graph's purpose is to prove that a country's growth has a direct relationship with climate change, signified by factors such as Fossil Fuel Consumption, CO2 Emission, and Electric Power Consumption.
